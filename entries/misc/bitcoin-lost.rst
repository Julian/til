============
Bitcoin Lost
============

An `analysis
<https://letstalkbitcoin.com/blog/post/rise-of-the-zombie-bitcoins>`_
[#]_ calculates that the fraction of bitcoin "permanently" lost (due
to e.g. wallets without keys, etc.) is around **25%** of current
circulation.
