Dunning-Kruger Isn’t a Thing

November 25, 2023

The Dunning-Kruger effect is effectively entirely debunked, and attributable to basic statistical error (though interestingly as of this moment in November 2023, on Wikipedia that information is limited to a mention on talk page). This article contains both a lengthier explanation of the mistake as well as links to papers in 2016, 2017 and 2020 which finally discovered it, trivial as it seems it was.
